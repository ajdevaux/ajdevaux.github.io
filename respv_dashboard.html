Influenza and Respiratory Syncytial Virus Prevalence in Swiss Wastewater


Graphs of the dPCR measurements of catchments of 6 different wastewater treatment plants (WWTPs) from around Switzerland. Values are normalized by flowrate and population.

  • Influenza A Virus (IAV) is indicated in red
  • Influenza B Virus (IBV) is in green
  • Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV) is in orange
Individual dots represent mean measurementsof 2 technical replicates, with the errorbar being the range of the two measurements. The line is the seven-day median of the values, requiring at least two data points within 7 days of each other to be plotted. Seven-day median can be turned off by clicking on the legend. Graph axes are fully interactive. A report and summary of these data can be found here.
